About Me

I was born in Newport Beach, CA. I grew up in Irvine. My favorite things to do are snorkeling, goin to the beach in general, eating sushi and ramen, and playing video games. I went to college at Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo and studied Food Science. I've done research and development in flavor chemistry and product development in snack foods. I was also a distiller for a distillery in Santa Barabara Country, making various whiskies and vodkas.

I am now studying Web Development at the UCLA extension program with the intention of finding a job in this field. While I am initially studying web development, I would ideally like to focus on web design. No matter what I do, I want to be able to create. I have ideas for developing my own websites and apps. I would like to eventually combine my background in the food industry with these new tech skills in some form.
